Natural Language Inference Judgments

We're interested in the rationale used by native English speakers to reach certain inferences. In this task, you will be presented with a sentence or phrase and asked to judge whether a second sentence or phrase follows from the first. You will also be asked to provide a brief explanation of your judgment: how did you arrive at your conclusion one way or another?
